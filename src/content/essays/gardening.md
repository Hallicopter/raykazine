Why you should cultivate your own corner of the internet. It's not about audience; it's about cultivation. Tending to your thoughts like plants unique to your own soil.

A digital garden is different from a blog. A blog is a stream of chronology. A garden is a topology of relevance. You update old posts. You prune dead links. You graft new ideas onto old stalks.
